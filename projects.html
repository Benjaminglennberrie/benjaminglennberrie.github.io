SmartFoods Nutrition (iOS App)

Nutrition Tracking & AI Integration

SmartFoods is an iOS app I developed using SwiftUI and Firebase. It helps users log their meals, visualize macronutrients in dynamic pie charts, and get personalized AI nutrition insights. The app integrates with Firebase Firestore for data storage and uses AI via a custom backend built on Cloud Run.

This personal project combines my passion for nutrition, mobile design, and AI-driven feedback systems.
